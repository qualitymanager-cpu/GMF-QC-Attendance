🔗 How to Share with Team
✅ Correct workflow — follow these steps in order:
Step 1. Add all your team members in Team Setup above
Step 2. Configure company settings, GPS coordinates & Google Sheets URL
Step 3. Click "⬇ Generate & Download Team File" below — this bakes all settings into a new file
Step 4. Host that downloaded file on GitHub Pages / Netlify / Google Sites
Step 5. Share the URL — team members will see their names immediately ✅
⚠️ Do NOT share the original downloaded file directly.
Always use "Generate & Download Team File" after making any changes — this ensures your latest team list is embedded in the file team members receive.
🌐 Free hosting options:
• Netlify Drop — drag & drop, instant URL (30 seconds, no account needed)
• GitHub Pages — free, permanent URL
• Google Sites — embed in a Google Site page

📱 Offline backup: Punches save locally too — auto-syncs to Google Sheet when back online.
